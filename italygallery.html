italy on film, 2018

the first film i ever shot was in the fall and winter of 2018, on my study abroad trip to venice, italy. a friend of mine handed mea canon ae-1 and a roll of black and white film before the trip and said, "enjoy!" i thought to myself it would be a fun little thing to play with, but i had my digital camera with me, i couldn't possibly shoot that much film while i was there. i was wrong.

i ended up shooting 8 rolls of film in 8 weeks.

this sequence of images, arranged in chronological order (to the best of my memory) spans the entire 8 weeks i lived in italy - arrival in milan and torino, daily life in venice, and trips to liverpool, murano, burano, and florence.

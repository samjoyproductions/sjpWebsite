i s o l a t i o n

this series, primarily shot on black & white film, was created during the COVID-19 epidemic between March and April 2020. i created this sequence of images to explore the concepts of isolation and individuality as they pertain to the human experience in such a weird time of global crisis. additionally, this series served as a way to document my life and experiences during this time, along with the few people i was thankful to be able to safely interact with.
